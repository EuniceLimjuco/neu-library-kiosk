How to Use the NEU Library Kiosk

Instructions and helpful tips

Kiosk Instructions

  1. Select your preferred sign-in method: Tap ID Card, Enter Student Number, or Institutional Google Email.
  2. If you choose Enter Student Number, type your full name and your 10-digit student number using your keyboard.
  3. If you choose Institutional Google Email, type your official @neu.edu.ph email using your keyboard.
  4. After signing in, choose your college or department.
  5. Proceed to the next page and select your purpose of visit.

Tips

  • Press Enter to continue quickly.
  • Press Backspace to delete one character.
  • Press Delete to clear the whole field.
  • Use your official and correct information to avoid failed check-in.
  • If the kiosk does not respond, ask the library staff for assistance.
