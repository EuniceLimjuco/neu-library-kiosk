How to Use the NEU Library Kiosk

Instructions and helpful tips

Kiosk Instructions

  1. Select your purpose of visit by clicking one of the options on the screen.
  2. Only one purpose can be selected at a time.
  3. Once selected, click Continue to proceed.
  4. Click Cancel if you want to go back to the home page.
  5. Make sure the selected purpose matches your actual reason for visiting the library.

Tips

  • Review your selected purpose before continuing.
  • If you selected the wrong option, just click another card to change it.
  • Ask library staff for help if you are unsure which purpose to choose.
